Typography


Intro

This is Turnip. Our brand typeface.

Turnip is the primary brand typeface for Harpy Airlines. An elegant serif infused with a quirky warmth, this headline face projects trustworthiness, optimism, and sincerity.


Roboto is our supporting font.

Graphik is our workhorse typeface, carefully chosen to support Turnip in more functional moments. Purposefully plain, this sans-serif prioritizes legibility and balances the expressiveness of Turnip.

Grid gutter

Our grid columns have a 16px padding on either side that results in a 32px gutter between columns and a 16px gutter on the grid edges. Even though our columns are fluid, the gutter remains constant.

Buttons


Basic Button

This button will be the most commonly used button on the site. 


            
<button class="button"><span>Book Now</span></button>

Animated Buttons with Arrow

This button will be used whenever you need to cycle through something such as, images, pages, or posts. 


            
<button class="button leftButton"><span>Left</span></button>
<button class="button rightButton"><span>Right</span></button>

Disabled and Loading Buttons

Whenever a button would be disabled, or if it is taking a while to load, then these would be the buttons that are be used. 


            
<button disabled class="disabledButton"><span>Disabled</span></button>
<button disabled class="loadingButton"><span>Loading</span></button>
