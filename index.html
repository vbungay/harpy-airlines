Typography


Intro

This is Turnip. Our brand typeface.

Turnip is the primary brand typeface for Harpy Airlines. An elegant serif infused with a quirky warmth, this headline face projects trustworthiness, optimism, and sincerity.


Roboto is our supporting font.

Graphik is our workhorse typeface, carefully chosen to support Turnip in more functional moments. Purposefully plain, this sans-serif prioritizes legibility and balances the expressiveness of Turnip.
